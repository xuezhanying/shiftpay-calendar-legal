Common Questions

How do I enter my roster?

Open the Calendar tab, tap a date, and choose Day Shift, Evening Shift, Night Shift, Day Off, or a custom shift.

Are pay estimates official payroll calculations?

No. ShiftPay Calendar provides estimates for personal planning. Always check your employer's payroll records and employment agreement.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the Me tab and tap Restore Purchases. Purchases are restored through your App Store account.

Does the app upload my roster?

The first version stores roster and pay settings locally on your device. iCloud sync is planned for a future version.
