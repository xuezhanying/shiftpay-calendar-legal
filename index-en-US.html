Why ShiftPay

Built for real rosters, not generic calendars.

Most calendar apps don't speak shift-worker. ShiftPay does. Penalty rates, public holiday loading, fortnightly cycles, payslip checks — wired in from day one.

📅

Roster at a glance

Day, evening, night and off — colour-coded across the month. Long-press to batch-enter weeks at a time.

💰

Pay estimator

Hourly rate plus night, Saturday, Sunday and public holiday multipliers. Get a credible monthly number before payday.

🔍

Payslip check

Enter what you actually got paid. We surface the gap, then explain likely reasons — missing penalty rates, holidays or overtime.

🔁

Roster templates

2 day / 2 night / 4 off, 4 on / 4 off, or your own custom cycle. Generate weeks of shifts in one tap.

🗓️

Public holiday aware

Built-in holiday data for AU, HK, MO, TW, CA and US — so holiday rates fire automatically on the right dates.

🔒

Local-first privacy

Your roster lives on your device. No account, no tracking, no selling your schedule. iCloud sync coming soon — opt-in only.
