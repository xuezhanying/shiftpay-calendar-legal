ShiftPay Calendar Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-28

ShiftPay Calendar helps shift workers record rosters and estimate pay. The first version is designed to keep your schedule and pay information on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your roster entries, pay rates, work patterns, or calendar notes.

We do not require an account for the first version of the app.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Shift entries.
  • Roster templates.
  • Hourly rate and pay rule settings.
  • Subscription entitlement state.

This data is used only to provide the app calendar, pay estimate, and Pro feature access.

Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

iCloud

iCloud sync is planned for a future version. If enabled later, your data may sync through your personal iCloud account according to Apple's iCloud terms.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@shiftpaycalendar.com
